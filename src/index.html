The goal of Web Developers Co-op (WDC) is to simplify the initial phases of starting a web development business by providing all the necessary forms, documentation, education, networking and advice all in one source for new business owners.

This is the place to find information and resources on creating a corporate entity, filing corporate taxes, creating a payroll and many others. We strive to connect our users to legal advisors, accounting services, and inform them of their inellectual property options. In addition, we help to curate a network of developers to discuss the subjects and tools pertinent to business creation.

For everyone on your team to advance through your business build, WDC will monitor their individual progress and verify that they have completed each step before moving on. This measured and verified method keeps your business development moving smoothly so that you can concentrate on the marvelous web architecture you intend to create.
