Setting up a co-op entity can be difficult. To smooth the process, we will link information you provide to pre-filled forms.

Please provide the following information regarding your new entity:

*All members of a co-op are shareholders, so it is not necessary to designate any shareholders.

If forming as LLC in your state, the group name must have the acronym "LLC" at the end of the title.

If forming LLC in the state of California, you cannot have the "cooperative" term in the group title.
